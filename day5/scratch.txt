------------------------------------------------
Input
------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------
Objective
------------------------------------------------------
 - Convert each seed number through other categories
   until soil number is found


------------------------------------------------------
Result
------------------------------------------------------
    LOWEST LOCATION NUMBER
   corresponding to any of
 the INITIAL SEEDS
